Welcome to the heart of Minot — where opportunity meets connection.

Live in Minot

Minot is a vibrant community with great schools and opportunities for families to thrive and grow together.

Oil & Gas Industry

Minot is located near the Bakken Formation, making it a central hub for oil production and supporting industries.

Rail & Distribution

Minot is a key railway location for transporting goods and agricultural products across the Midwest.

Why Minot?

  • Minot is home to the North Dakota State Fair.
  • Minot hosts the largest Scandinavian festival in North America — the Norsk Høstfest.
  • Minot's Oak Park was voted “America’s Favorite Park” and won a $100,000 grant from Coca-Cola.

What We Do

The Minot Chamber of Commerce is more than a business network — it’s a community foundation. We empower local businesses by providing advocacy, visibility, and growth resources tailored to entrepreneurs and companies of all sizes. Our members benefit from mentorship opportunities, professional development events, and strategic connections that drive long-term success. Whether you're launching a startup or expanding a legacy company, our mission is to help you thrive in Minot's growing economic landscape.
